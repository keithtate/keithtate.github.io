Say Hello

We would love for you to share your photos of Keith with us, so please don't hesitate to attach some to an email and send them our way. For a collection of photos, or perhaps some videos, you will likely need to use a service like Google Drive due to the maximum allowable size for email attachments being quite limited. If you don't have a Gmail account, you can try iCloud, Dropbox, OneDrive, or MEGA instead (they all provide ways to send us an email link to a file or folder that you'd like to share). And finally, a service named Send that does not require account registration (or any of these Send instances) -- just email us the link you receive after uploading the file.

To say hello, Keith's wife and son can be emailed at tatefamily@duck.com.

Thank you!
